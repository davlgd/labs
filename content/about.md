I've been writing this blog since 2023. Most posts come out of something I'm actually working on — a packaging chore that turned into a deep-dive, a language I'm trying out, a kernel feature I just learned existed.

I'm a fan of **small, comprehensible systems**. Distros that fit in your head. Languages whose compilers tell you the truth. Tools that do one thing without three layers of abstraction. A lot of what I write is, in some way, an argument for that.

If you want to chat, the fediverse is the easiest way. The RSS feed is also there, doing its quiet little job. **No tracking, no analytics, no popovers.**
